Global Weather Patterns

Weather Trio: Humidity, Cloud Cover & Precipitation

One significant finding is the positive correlation between humidity, precipitation, and cloud cover . This shows a fundammental aspect of the Earth's water cycle, where higher humidity leads to the condensation of water vapor into clouds, eventually resulting in precipitation.

Tropical climate zones including parts of Africa, Central and South America , the Philippines, and Micronesia experience warm temperatures and high levels of moisture throughout the year. This climate pattern contributes to lush vegetation, diverse ecosystems, and unique weather patterns, which are of ecological and cultural significance.
